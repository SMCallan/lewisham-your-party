**What you need to know**

- ﻿This initial phase of strike action is running for three days may 6* to Friday 8th
- ﻿GMB UNION apologises for any disruption the strike may cause to cleaning and meals.
- ﻿GMB members do not want to strike but they are being sacked and their hours are being reduced for cost saving.
- ﻿GMB UNIION has requested risk assessments for the restructuring from the TRUST and

OCS but these have not been provided.

- ﻿Our members are deeply concerned about the impact of a 'slash and burn' approach to catering and cleaning services across SLaM and the impact this will have on patients and visitors to the hospital.
- ﻿We are aware that some patients are detained under section and our members believe that they have a right to have good food and clean surroundings to aid their re-

covery.
