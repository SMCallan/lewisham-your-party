The wait is over. Today, we are proud to launch the official, volunteer-built digital home for the Lewisham branch of Your Party. As the national central committee formally establishes our party structure, we know that the real work doesn't wait for bureaucracy. The fight for our community is happening right now, on the streets of Lewisham. We needed a dedicated hub to organize local campaigning, coordinate street action, and fight back against the gentrification pushing working people out of our borough. So, we built one ourselves.

## **Built for the People, by the People**

This website is a reflection of our core political philosophy. It wasn't built by an expensive corporate PR agency, and it isn't running on bloated software that tracks your every move to sell to data brokers. Our digital infrastructure is 100% volunteer-coded, using a lightning-fast, zero-budget grassroots tech stack. We don't use marketing cookies, we don't track you across the internet, and we respect your privacy. Every ounce of energy here is focused on one thing: **organising.**

## **What You Can Do Here**

**Join the Branch:** Use the giant form on the homepage to get on our secure local mailing list.

**Find Local Events:** Check the "Events" and "Street Action" feeds to see where we are leafleting, holding stalls, or meeting up next.

**Stay Informed:** Keep an eye on our "Announcements" for hyper-local news and campaign updates.

We are building a new kind of political party—one that actually belongs to us. We stand for homes for people, not profit, and we are ready to take that message to every doorstep in Lewisham SE6 and beyond. But a website is just a tool. The real power of this movement is you.

## **Sign up on the homepage, share this site with your neighbors, and let's get to work.**
