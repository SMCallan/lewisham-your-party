# MARCH TOGETHER AGAINST THE FAR RIGHT

We're joining the Together Alliance, a broad coalition of activists, public figures and organisations, together for a national march against the hate and division of the far right.

## SATURDAY, 28 MARCH

*   **12pm: Main march assembles at Park Lane**
    
*   **1pm: Main march leaves Park Lane AND Short route assembles at Trafalgar Square/Whitehall**
    
*   **2pm: Front of main march arrives Trafalgar Square/Whitehall**
    
*   **2pm: Speeches start on stages**
    
*   **3pm: Music event starts in Trafalgar**
    
*   **6pm: Event finishes**
